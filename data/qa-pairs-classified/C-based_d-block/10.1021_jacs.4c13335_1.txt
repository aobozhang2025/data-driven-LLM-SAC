question
Preparation of single atom Fe doped by 4 N and 1 Cl, supported on nanozyme

answer
The precursor is prepared as followed:
1. Firstly, we prepared a mixed solvent by mixing DMF and methanol with the volume  ratio of 4:1 (VDMF: Vmethanol = 4:1). 4276 mg Zn(NO3)2Â·6H2O and 2032 mg Fe(acac)3  was added into 60 ml mixed solvent (VDMF: Vmethanol = 4:1) in flask A and the mixture was under ultrasound for several minutes in order to obtain a homogeneous solution.  
2. 4644 mg 2-methylimidazole was added into 40 ml the mixed solvent (VDMF: Vmethanol =  4:1) in flask B under ultrasonic treatment for several minutes. 
3. Then, the solution in  flask A was mixed with the solution in flask B at room temperature and the mixed  solution was stirred violently for 24 h.
4. After crystallization of Fe(acac)3@ZIF-8, we collected Fe(acac)3@ZIF-8 powder by centrifugation at 15,000 rpm for 5 min and washed the product by methanol for six times. 
5. Finally, we dried the obtained  Fe(acac)3@ZIF-8 powder in a oven at 50 oC for several hours.  

The catalyst is synthesized with metal precursor Fe(acac)3 and support ZIF-8:
1. we put 3 g NaCl in a smaller ceramic boat separately. 
2. Subsequently, 3 g NaCl in a smaller ceramic boat and 150 mg Fe(acac)3@ZIF-8 powder were put together in a larger ceramic boat with a lid.
3. We put the NaCl in the upstream of Fe(acac)3@ZIF-8. 
4. Before pyrolysis, the air in the tube  furnace was removed by a vacuum pump and then the argon gas (99.999%) was input into the tube furnace. The above treatment was repeated three times. 
5. We heated the sample to 900oC with a heating rate of 5 oC/min and the temperature was kept at 900 oC  for 2.5 h under flowing argon gas (99.999%). 
6. After pyrolysis, the Fe-N4Cl/CNCl  SAzyme was cooled to room temperature naturally.
