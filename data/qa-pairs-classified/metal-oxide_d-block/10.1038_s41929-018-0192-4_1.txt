question
synthesis of single atom Ir supported on MgAl2O4

answer
The catalyst is prepared impregnation with support MgAl2O4

The catalyst is prepared as followed:
1. The MgAl2O4 support (Puralox MG 28, Sasol) was calcined at 500 째C for 4 h before impregnation. 
2. Deionized water (20 ml) was added to a Pyrex bottle and the pH was adjusted with nitric acid to 2.8. 
3. The Ir nitrate precursor (8.7 wt% Ir, Furuya Metal Co.) was added into the pH-adjusted solution. 
4. The MgAl2O4 support was then added into the solution while stirring. The final pH of the solution was 9.5. The nominal Ir weight loading was 0.0025%. 
5. After 12 h of stirring, the solution was filtered out and the solid sample was dried in air for 24 h at room temperature. 
6. The sample was then dried at 80 째C and 100 째C before calcination at 500 째C for 4 h. 
7. The Ir in the solution after filtration was measured by inductively coupled plasma atomic emission spectroscopy and no Ir was detected.
