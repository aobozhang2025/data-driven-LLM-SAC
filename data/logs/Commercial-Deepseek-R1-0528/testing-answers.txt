  - 2-Methylimidazole (0.328 g, 4 mmol)  
  - Methanol (MeOH, 40 mL total)  
- **Procedure**:  
  1. Dissolve Zn(NO₃)₂·6H₂O (0.297 g) in 20 mL MeOH (Solution A).  
  2. Dissolve 2-methylimidazole (0.328 g) in 20 mL MeOH (Solution B).  
  3. Rapidly mix Solution A and Solution B under stirring (500 rpm) at 25°C.  
  4. React for 1 hour at 25°C.  
  5. Age without stirring for 24 hours at 25°C.  
  6. Centrifuge at 8,000 rpm for 10 minutes. Discard supernatant.  
  7. Wash precipitate with MeOH (3 × 20 mL), centrifuging at 8,000 rpm for 5 minutes each time.  
  8. Dry solids in a vacuum oven at 60°C for 12 hours. Obtain ZIF-8 powder (white).  

**Step 2: Incorporation of Single-Atom Mn via Solid-State Diffusion**  
- **Chemicals**:  
  - ZIF-8 powder (100 mg, from Step 1)  
  - Manganese(II) acetate tetrahydrate (Mn(CH₃COO)₂·4H₂O, 0.0123 g, 0.05 mmol)  
  - Ethanol (EtOH, 10 mL)  
- **Procedure**:  
  1. Dissolve Mn(CH₃COO)₂·4H₂O (0.0123 g) in EtOH (10 mL).  
  2. Add ZIF-8 powder (100 mg) to the solution. Sonicate for 10 minutes.  
  3. Stir at 25°C for 6 hours (500 rpm).  
  4. Evaporate solvent at 60°C under reduced pressure (rotary evaporator).  
  5. Transfer solid to a quartz boat.  
  6. Heat in a tube furnace under N₂ flow (50 sccm):  
     - Ramp from 25°C to 350°C at 5°C/min.  
     - Hold at 350°C for 2 hours.  
     - Cool to 25°C at 10°C/min.  
  7. Obtain Mn-doped ZIF-8 (gray powder).  

**Step 3: Acid Etching to Isolate Single Mn Atoms**  
- **Chemicals**:  
  - Mn-doped ZIF-8 (100 mg, from Step 2)  
  - Dilute HCl (0.1 M, 20 mL)  
  - Deionized water (DIW, 50 mL)  
- **Procedure**:  
  1. Suspend Mn-doped ZIF-8 in 0.1 M HCl (20 mL).  
  2. Stir at 25°C for 1 hour (500 rpm).  
  3. Centrifuge at 10,000 rpm for 10 minutes. Discard supernatant.  
  4. Wash with DIW (3 × 20 mL), centrifuging at 10,000 rpm for 5 minutes each time.  
  5. Dry solids in a vacuum oven at 60°C for 12 hours. Obtain final product (Mn single atoms on Zn support).
